Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a rental property?

Browse our Listings page, use search and filters to narrow down by location, property type, and price. Click any listing to see full details and contact the owner directly via WhatsApp.

Is Smart Rent Universe free to use?

Yes! Browsing and contacting owners is completely free. Property owners get 10 free listings on our Starter plan. The Pro plan (PKR 999/month) allows up to 100 listings and adds featured placement.

How do I post my property?

Create a free account, go to "Post Listing", fill in your property details (title, description, price, location, type, WhatsApp number), and submit. Your listing goes live instantly.

What is a Featured Listing?

Featured listings appear at the top of search results and on the homepage, giving your property maximum visibility. Featured placement is available on the Pro plan.

How do I upgrade to Pro?

Go to your Dashboard → Upgrade Plan. Send the payment via EasyPaisa or JazzCash to our official numbers, fill out the payment form, and our team will verify and activate your Pro account within 24 hours.

Is my personal data secure?

Absolutely. We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore with strict security rules. Only you can access and edit your own listings and profile data.

About Smart Rent Universe

Smart Rent Universe helps people find rental homes and shops easily in Pakistan while helping property owners connect with genuine tenants. We believe renting should be simple, transparent, and direct — no middlemen, no hidden fees.

Our Mission

Pakistan's rental market has long been dominated by agents and informal word-of-mouth. We built Smart Rent Universe to digitize and democratize this process — giving both renters and landlords a powerful, free platform to connect on their own terms. Whether you're a young professional looking for an apartment in Karachi or a shopkeeper searching for commercial space in Lahore, we've made the process faster and more trustworthy than ever before.
